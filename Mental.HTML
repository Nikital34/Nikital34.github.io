Mental Health


Mental health is a crucial aspect of a physical well-being. If you've been feeling different lately, you're in the right place.



What is mental health?

Mental health is our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. Lots of variables go into a mental health of a person.



Why is mental health important?

  • Affects how we feel
  • Affects how we think
  • Affects how we act
  • Determines how we handle our situations


What are some early warning signs?

  • Changes in sleeping and eating
  • Hearing voices
  • Thinking of self-harm
  • Feeling hopeless



How to get help?

You can always seek help by finding professionals in your area through a Google search. You can also call the following hotlines:

  • Suicide Hotline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Mental Illness Hotline: 1-800-950-6264
  • General Crisis Hotline: Text SUPPORT to 741-741
