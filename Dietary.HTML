Dietary


This is the Dietary Section of QuestWell. This part of the website is the promotion of different tasty lifestyles. Here we well three different kinds of healthy diets.



What are Plant-Based Diets?

Plant-Based Diets are kinds of diets that rely on mainly the consumption of plants rather than animals.



Why would this Diet be for you?

  • Easier weight management
  • Eating plants based can decrease risks of depression, anxiety, and brain fog
  • Plant based diets have been linked to higher a lifespan
  • Plants based diets leave a lighter environment footprint


Cons of the Plant-Based Diet

  • Potential for nutrient deficiencies
  • You have to prepare your food more often
  • Adapting to the new diet might be challenging and time consume


Vegetarian Diet




What is Vegetarianism?

Vegetarianism is a diet that practices the avoidance of meat consumption



What can Vegetarians not eat?

In short, it depend of the kind of vegetarian: Lacto-ovo-vegetarian diet: Eliminates meat, fish and poultry but allows eggs and dairy products.



  • Lacto-vegetarian diet: Eliminates meat, fish, poultry and eggs but allows dairy products.
  • Lacto-vegetarian diet: Eliminates meat, fish, poultry and eggs but allows dairy products.
  • Ovo-vegetarian diet: Eliminates meat, fish, poultry and dairy products but allows eggs

Benefits to specifically vegetarianism

  • Possibly causes weight loss
  • Boost heart health
  • May stabilize blood sugar

Food guide and tips for Vegans and Vegetarians


Meat Substitutes

  • Tofu
  • Beans and sprouts
  • Nuts
  • Potatoes
  • Jackfruit
  • Cauliflower
  • Store-bought vegan beef and chicken

Dairy substitutes for Vegans and Vegetarians :

  • Almond milk
  • Oat milk
  • Coconut milk- Replacement for heavy creams
  • Soy milk
  • Vegan cheeses

  • Oats
